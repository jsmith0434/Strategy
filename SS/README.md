# 📊 Strategic Pricing for Non-Profit Growth (SS)

### 🌟 Project Overview
This project was a volunteer strategic initiative designed to stabilize and scale a high-impact NGO. By moving from "guesswork" to a data-driven pricing model, the organization was able to optimize its revenue streams, providing the financial runway needed to expand community services and solidify its long-term business plan.

> **Privacy Note:** All identifying information, specific locations, and stakeholder names have been removed or obfuscated to preserve organizational integrity and privacy.

---

### 🛠️ Methodology & Technical Approach
To provide the NGO with a professional-grade strategy, the following analytical steps were taken:

* **Competitive Intelligence**: Conducted a manual survey and normalization of pricing data across direct and indirect competitors.
* **Statistical Analysis (R)**: Utilized **Linear Regression** and **Elasticity Modeling** to predict how price changes would impact service volume.
* **Revenue Optimization**: Identified the "Value-Based" pricing ceiling that maximized margin without alienating the community the NGO serves.
* **Deliverable Synthesis**: Translated raw statistical outputs into a non-technical executive deck for board-level decision-making.
