
# 📈 Strategy & Business Intelligence

A collection of data-driven strategy projects, competitive frameworks, and financial modeling examples. This repository demonstrates the application of quantitative analysis to high-level business decision-making.

## 📂 Featured Projects

### 💰 [Pricing Strategy & Elasticity Analysis]

**Objective:** Determine the optimal price point for service offerings by balancing market positioning with revenue maximization.

  * **Methodology:** \* **Competitor Benchmarking:** Scraped and normalized pricing data from top-tier competitors to establish a market baseline.
      * **Linear Regression Modeling:** Developed a predictive model to estimate demand sensitivity and identify the "sweet spot" on the price-demand curve.
      * **Optimization:** Identified the price point that maximizes Total Contract Value (TCV) without sacrificing market share.
  * **Tools Used:** Python (Pandas, Scikit-Learn), Excel (Solver), Competitive Intelligence Frameworks.
